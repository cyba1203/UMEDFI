CCAPS presents at KAPA International Summer Conference 2024

University of Makati participated in the 2024 Korean Public Administration Summer Conference and Annual Korean Association of Public Administration (KAPA) International Conference with the theme, “Navigating Change: Equity, Leadership, Community and Robust Governance” on June 19-21, at the Yeosu Expo Convention Center in Jeollanam-do, Busan, South Korea.

UMak representatives participate on PSPA International Conference

The Philippine Society for Public Administration (PSPA) conducted their annual International Conference in collaboration with Eastern Regional Organization for Public Administration (EROPA), University of the Philippines National College of Public Administration and Governance (UP NCPAG), Ateneo School of Government and Carlos Hilado Memorial State University on December 5-7, 2024, where the faculty from the College of Continuing, Advanced and Professional Studies (CCAPS) represented the university.

UMak moves towards enhanced Public Administration Pedagogy

The University of Makati (UMak) Vice President for Academic Affairs and Dean of College for Continuing, Advanced and Professional Studies (CCAPS) Dr. Ederson D.T. Tapia, the program directors: Dr. Rhene C. Tabajen, Dr. Sonia F. Pimentel, Professor Pompeyo C. Adamos II and Professor Felizardo A. UMak educators engage in the 5-day PAhayag Masterclass

The College of Continuing, Advanced and Professional Studies (CCAPS) joined the PAhayag: Masterclass Seminar Series formed by the University of the Philippines National College of Public Administration and Governance (UP-NCPAG), Governance Reform, Innovation and Transformation Research Laboratories (GRIT Labs), Association of Schools of Public Administration of the Philippines (ASPAP) Inc. and Philippine Society for Public Administration (PSPA) at the NCPAG Assembly Hall, UP Diliman last February 19-21, 2025.

The Inno Voyage: Navigating Smart Futures in Industrialization, Education, Governance, and Smart City Development is an initiative that brought together global leaders, entrepreneurs, and stakeholders to explore advancements in smart city development.

University of Makati Recognized Among Top Global Innovators at 2024 WURI Conference

OHeld at WURI Global Conference 2024 in Seoul, South Korea from September 23-26, the University of Makati (UMak) has earned a significant achievement by securing the 209th spot in the 2024 World University Rankings for Innovation (WURI). This recognition highlights UMak's dedication to innovation and excellence in higher education, standing out among 1,072 institutions globally.
