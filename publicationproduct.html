Two new knowledge products launched at NCPAG’s Better Governance Forum No. 6

The University of the Philippines - National College of Public Administration and Governance (UP-NCPAG) together with the Eastern Regional Organization for Public Administration (EROPA) and the Philippine Society for Public Administration (PSPA) launched two new knowledge products during the Better Governance Forum No.6: The State of Local Governance and Innovations in the Philippines on June 28, 2024, at the International Center for Public Administration - National College of Public Administration and Governance (ICPA-NCPAG).

The two knowledge products titled “Innovations and Excellence: Understanding Local Governance in the Philippines (2nd Edition)” by Professor Emeritus of UP-NCPAG and Secretary-General of EROPA, Dr. Alex Brillantes Jr. and Librarian of UP-NCPAG, Mr. Karl Emmanuel Ruiz was officially presented to the public.

Furthemore, the 2nd knowledge product titled, “Phronesis, Knowledge Creation and Public Administration: Three Cases on Amalgamation and Transportation in the Philippines” by Professor Emeritus of UP-NCPAG and Secretary-General of EROPA, Dr. Alex Brillantes Jr. and Dean, Graduate School of Public and Development Management, Development Academy of the Philippines, Dr. Lizan Perante-Calina was also launched on the said date. The printing and publication of the knowledge books were supported by the UMak Educational Development Foundation’s financial assistance program.
