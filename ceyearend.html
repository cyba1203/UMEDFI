University of Makati - Year-end Convocation 2024

Themed: “Joyful Jubilee: A Holiday Toast to Achievements of Working Together”

The University of Makati celebrated its Annual Year-End Convocation on December 11, 2024, with the theme "Joyful Jubilee: A Holiday Toast to Achievements of Working Together." The event featured a heartfelt holiday toast, honoring the university's accomplishments over the past years.

A highlight of the event was the much-anticipated raffle, donated by the UMak Education Development Foundation, Inc., which brought joy and appreciation to the academic and non-academic personnel.

The raffles served as tokens of recognition for the personnel's dedication throughout the year. More than just a celebration, the year-end convocation fostered a sense of unity and motivation, inspiring everyone to look forward to another successful year ahead.
