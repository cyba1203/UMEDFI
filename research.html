Philippine Education Leaders Embark on U.S. Benchmarking Mission to Enhance Education Sector

The delegation of Philippine education leaders, including university presidents, commissioners, and policymakers, successfully concluded an Executive Benchmarking Mission in Washington DC and Boston, Massachusetts, United States of America from April 15 to 19, 2024.

UMak delegates present their papers at the 1st KaMaG-Aral National Conference

University of Makati delegates from the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences (CLAS) and College of Innovative Teacher Education (CITE) attended the 1st KaMaG-Aral National Conference organized by the Social Sciences Research and Extension Institute (SSREI) of the College of Social Sciences, University of the Philippines (UP) Baguio, the Katipunan ng mga Mananaliksik at Guro ng Araling Panlipunan (KaMaG-Aral), with the theme, “Social Studies Education in the Post-Pandemic Era”
